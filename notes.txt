Maria owned a Toyota Camry in Houston, Texas, reliable always.
During Hurricane Harvey, her city was flooded with powerful rain.
Her neighbors evacuated while Maria stayed behind caring for others.
Floodwaters reached her Camry, submerging it to the windows.
After water receded, Maria hoped her Camry would still work.
She dried it carefully, jumped the battery, and tried again.
Miraculously, the engine started, and relief spread through her family.
The Camry helped them drive to safety repeatedly during crisis.
Neighbors witnessed the resilience and called it "The Survivor" proudly.
Maria’s Toyota proved strong, reliable—even after such overwhelming adversity.
